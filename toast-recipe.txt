Ingredients:
 - 1 slice of bread
 - 1 tsp butter

Directions:
 1. Set the toasting setting to preferred browning level.
 2. Insert slice of bread in the first toaster slot.
 3. Press down the carriage control level until it locks into the down position.
 4. When the toasting cycle is complete, the toaster will push the toasted bread to the top
    by quickly raising the bread carriage.
 5. Remove the toasted bread and place on a small plate.
 6. Use a butter knife to spread the 1 tsp of butter over the toasted bread while still warm.
 7. Optional - For toasy bites, cut the toasted bread into 16 equally size squares.
